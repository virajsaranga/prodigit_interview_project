Career Counselling

We have scheduled counselling sessions for interested candidates who have their plans to study abroad. The initial counselling enables us to understand the career graph of students.

University Selection

University Selection is a major step towards garnering student’s career graph. Our in-house team of mentors gives a pre-university briefing for students and acquaint them with the right university.

Scholarship Assistance

When students apply for a particular university we make sure that we accustom the students with scholarship assistance being provided by the university.

Course Selection

The expert panel of Consultants are always available for the students who find it tough to select their course with universities when applying for their graduate and postgraduate courses.

Visa Assistance

The students who enrol with us have a privilege to have a comprehensive visa assistance and documentation required for the successful admission to the university.

Pre-Departure Assistance

We are also involved in the pre-departure assistance service for the students. The in-house team brief the students about their travel plans, accommodation and other amenities.
